Member FAQs

  • These funds will be used for a variety of reasons, such as supplies for game days, special member-only events, merchandise, and additional operating costs. All of these funds will be reinvested back into the Sirens Supporters Group for the benefit of its members or be donated to agreed upon organizations. The Sirens Leadership team is comprised of unpaid volunteers and will not be paid from these funds.

  • The Black Women’s Player Collective (BWPC) is a non-for profit 501(c)(3) organization created by the black players in the National Women’s Soccer League (NWSL) to advance opportunities for black girls in sport and beyond.

    The National Women’s Soccer League Players Association (NWSLPA) is the official union of active players in the NWSL.

    Both of these organizations strive to create safe and inclusive environments for soccer players everywhere. The Sirens are happy to support them in their missions.

  • We’re sorry to hear that you would like to cancel your membership. Memberships can be canceled at any time – unfortunately, memberships are nonrefundable. Please feel free to email us at membership.sirens@gmail.com with any feedback that you would like to share.

SIRENS Philosophy

SUPPORT the players, staff, and club - on and off the field, match day or not.

INVITE you (and you and you and you) to join us. Everyone is welcome in The Cove!

RESPECT all individuals and recognize that their bodies have different needs.; There is no one way to support the team; we just want you here!

ENCOURAGE the love, awareness, and spread of professional soccer, particularly for those who have been underrepresented including women, trans, and non-binary individuals, locally, nationally, and globally.

NURTURE a proactive, inclusive community that contributes to, engages with, and builds up a spirit that is bigger than each of us.

STAND UP against acts of hatred or abuse. Discrimination towards any group or individuals is antithetical to our purpose and will not be tolerated in The Cove.
